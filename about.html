INFO

Saarni Music was founded in 2016 by Markus Alavire (FKA Heinonen), a musician since 1999 and a passionate recording engineer since 2004. The mission was to create a Studio and a Record Label that would have the emphasis on musicality and humanity, and a commitment to quality. This mission has continued ever since and is growing more relevant each year. With this demand, Saarni Music has expanded the usage of the studio and the kind of work we do.

Examples of the types of projects we have worked on (but not limited to):
Studio: Single, EP and Album Recording, Producing, Mixing, Mastering
Live: Live show Organizing, Mixing, Recording
Photo: Photo shoots for Promo pictures, Music video
Creation: Songwriting, Lyrics, Arrangement, Preproduction
Recruiting session musicians
Voice-over talent recruiting, script translation, recording, editing
Private lessons and consulting
