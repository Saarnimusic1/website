Music Production for Individuals

Saarni Music is an all-around faculty serving musicians in all stages of their creativity.

SERVICES

RECORDING

Saarni Studio is a very versatile recording environment. Our 45m2 acoustically treated live room works perfectly for tight drum sounds, clean full band live recordings, acoustic instruments and anything outside and in between.

MIXING

Saarni Studio has a great room for mixing and mastering and years of experience ranging from mellow songs with a few tracks to massive projects with aggressive sounds. All services available in the studio or Online.

MUSIC PRODUCTION

Sometimes you as an artist will create the perfect lyric, the catchiest melody ever or a song that has it all. Full potential of the song can be harnessed by collaborating with the right producer.

MASTERING

Mastering is the finishing touch for a song to make sure it sounds release ready.

RENTAL SPACE

Saarni Studio can be rented out for various activities and events.

TEACHING & CONSULTATION

We are willing to share a lifetime of knowledge through private lessons and consultations for groups.
